All about African Dog Breeds

As africans we never really give alot of mind to the dogs we see along the road,
at our homes or even the ones at country sides.
But every time we see a dog breed from foreign countries then all our attention
is drawn to them and all over sudden everyone wants to pat them.
Then the strangest thing happens and now at the back of everyones mind everyone wants
to own a dog.We are here to help
